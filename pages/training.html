Training

The Computational Molecular Biology Unit at UAB is actively promoting the development of courses for training students in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. In collaboration with other researcher at UAB, we are working to establish a Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (CB2) program at UAB. For more details please visit the CB2 webpage.

Currently we submitted for approval the following courses in Pathology that will be cross linked to GBS and open to students of the 8 themes. The draft and information about those courses are linked below.

  • CB2-101 Introduction to Scientific Computing (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti
  • CB2-201 Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti
  • GBS787 - Computational Genomics (3 Credits). Instructor: Malay Basu.
  • GBS748 - Structural Bioinformatics (3 Credits). Instructor: Emidio Capriotti.
Students with research interests in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics and in those offered courses please contact Malay Basu or Emidio Capriotti.
